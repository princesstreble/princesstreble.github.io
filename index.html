Music is a good Friend to Everyone

Mozart Effect

Music plays an important role in many aspects of our lives. It can be inspirational, motivational, devotional, educational and can have many other traits including being magical, especially for young children.

Numerous psychological studies have long concluded that there is a relationship between music and its impact on spatial reasoning. This is known as the “Mozart Effect” and it can, for everyone including young children, espouse in improved problem solving skills; improved social behaviours and interaction with others; and an increased rate of learning of anything.

Exciting way of learning music theory

Young minds will learn the basics of music theory without even realizing it. Children will learn while having fun with the magical and adorable characters of Melody Town. The Princess Treble series of applications will keep young minds entertained and engaged, and at the same time, enhance their learning skills on music theory

Welcome to the world of Princess Treble

Princess Treble

A long time ago, evil spells took Princess Treble away from Melody Town. But there was one thing that not even spells could affect – her love for music.
Now, Princess Treble is back with one goal – to bring the music back to her beloved Kingdom.

She teaches children not only music theory but also important life lessons in kindness, compassion, and support for others, and the spirit of working together.
